OUR STORY

in 1960, Elsharkia Co. was established as a leader in selling and manually producing for painting materials through the major local agents for European manufacturers, beside importing painting products of worldwide brands.

Visit Us More Details

Who We Are

Elsharkia Group as one of Egyptian leaders in painting and chemical industries, performing many activities such as:
- Importing and delivering Raw materials for chemicals and coating.
- Manufacturing and delivering Panting materials.
- Agent for most of painting vendors in market.
- Providing Consulting and construction services for customers and major specialized companies in the field.
